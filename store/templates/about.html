About us

Welcome to Shopper, your number one source for all things. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of products, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2017 we have come a long way from its beginnings in a small store. When store first started out, the only goal was, providing the best equipment for fellow customers and that drove us to do intense research and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
